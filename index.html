How to Play


  1. Choose a Difficulty level (Easy, Intermediate, Hard).
  2. Click the "Play" button to start the Game.
  3. Guess a number within the given range (e.g 1 - 100).
  4. Enter your guess in the input field.
  5. Click "Check" to submit your answer.
  6. The game will tell you if your guess is higher or lower than the target number.
  7. Keep guessing until you correctly guess the target number.
  8. The Game ends when you win or run out of Lifelines.

Ready to give it a try? Click the "Play" button to start.
